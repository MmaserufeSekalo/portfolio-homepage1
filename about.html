Mmaserufe Sekalo

Mmaserufe Sekalo, is young creative born and raised in the northern province, Limpopo, South Africa. Mmaserufe graduated with a degree in agriculture before going into the multi facetted creative work. Since graduation, Mmaserufe enjoyed living on the road for work, which involved mostly local travel and international travel until June 2022.

Outside work, Mmaserufe enjoys meeting new people, running and playing games

Some of Mmaserufe's achievement include recent nomination for World Food Programme Summit 2022 as Aafrica's Brightest Young Mind intended formulate solutions to Feed the Future, in partnership with BYM.
